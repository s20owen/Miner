System Panel

Desktop: move with `WASD` or arrows, aim with the mouse, hold click to fire, `Space` to dash, `Q` to switch weapon.

Mobile: left stick moves, right stick aims and fires, and the dash pill gives a quick burst away from blocks.

Install: add Orbit Mine to your home screen to run it as a landscape PWA on iPhone.
